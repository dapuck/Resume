Education

Course Work

The courses that I took at IIT included Operating Systems, Technical Communications, Data Structures, and Software Engineering. For my minor I also had to take several courses on web technologies, such as Rich Internet Applications, Internet Technologies & Web Design, and Service-Oriented Architectures. For my specialization I had to take Cryptography and Network Security, Information Security, Database Organization, and Data Communications.

About the Illinois Institute of Technology

The Illinois Institute of Technology is a Ph.D.-granting research university with programs in engineering, architecture, the sciences, humanities, psychology, business, law, and design.

IIT was founded in 1940 after the merging of the Armour Institute of Technology and the Lewis Institute. IIT has an interdisciplinary approach to education, including the Interprofesional Projects Program (IPro).
